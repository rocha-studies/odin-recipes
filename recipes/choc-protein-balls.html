Chocolate Protein Balls


Easy protein ball recipe using chocolate protein powder, natural peanut butter, oats, and seeds.
Satisfy your sweet tooth with chocolate protein balls that'll please even the pickiest of eaters.
Loaded with healthy ingredients and tons of irresistible flavor, this protein ball recipe is perfect for on-the-go snackers looking for a delicious and nutritious treat.
Preparation time: 10 min. | Cook time: 30 min. | Total time: 40 min. | Servings: 10

Ingredients

  • Rolled oats: 1 cup.
  • Peanut butter: 1/2 cup.
  • Honey: 1/3 cup.
  • Dark chocolate: 1/4 cup, chopped.
  • Flax seeds: 2 tablespoons.
  • Chia seeds: 2 tablespoons.
  • Protein powder: 1 tablespoon, chocolate-flavored, or to taste.

Directions:

  1. Combine oats, peanut butter, honey, chocolate, flax seeds, chia seeds, and protein powder in a bowl until evenly mixed. Cover the bowl with plastic wrap; refrigerate at least 30 minutes.
  2. Scoop chilled mixture into balls. Keep cold until serving.
  3. Enjoy!
